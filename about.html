Our Founder

Rolex Tabutol

Rolex Tabutol, born on November 17, 1976, in the Philippines, is the visionary force behind Lex Luxe Transports. His journey in the luxury transportation industry started in 2015 with Pinoy Paris Cars, where he began with just one Mercedes Benz to offer tours in Paris, France, and Western Europe. Through dedication and passion, he earned the trust of nearly 50 recurring and loyal clients, setting the foundation for an extraordinary future.

With a commitment to excellence, Rolex transformed Pinoy Paris Cars into Lex Luxe Transports, becoming a symbol of elegance and unparalleled service in luxury transport. As a devoted father to four wonderful kids, his family-oriented perspective reflects in every aspect of our service.

At Lex Luxe Transports, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch experiences, whether it's a guided tour, a fairytale wedding, a productive business trip, or a delightful tourism expedition. With our expanding team and Rolex's passion for extraordinary travel experiences, our company is poised to continue its ascent as a leading name in luxury transportation. Welcome aboard as we redefine the art of travel with elegance and sophistication.
