Castle Package Itinerary : France and Germany


Day 1: Arrival in Paris - Transfer to Loire Valley

• Welcome to Paris, where our private chauffeur awaits your arrival.

• Embark on a luxurious transfer to the mesmerizing Loire Valley in one of our opulent Mercedes Benz Class V vans.

• Optional: Immerse yourself in the grandeur of a stay at an Airbnb chateau, embracing the ambiance of a bygone era.


Day 2: Loire Valley Castle Tour

• Embrace a full-day guided tour of the most magnificent chateaux in the Loire Valley.

• Explore iconic castles like Château de Chambord, Château de Chenonceau, and Château de Blois.

• Savor a gourmet lunch at a prestigious French restaurant, complementing the regal experience.

• Return to your luxurious accommodation, filled with fond memories of the day's enchanting escapades.


Day 3: Loire Valley Wine Experience

• Delight in a visit to renowned wineries for an unforgettable wine-tasting experience.

• Indulge in a leisurely lunch at a charming vineyard restaurant, basking in the region's gastronomic delights.

• Choose to continue exploring castles or picturesque villages like Amboise or Saumur, as per your desire.

• Optional: Engage in a cooking class, learning the secrets of French culinary artistry.


Day 4: Transfer to Versailles

• Bid farewell to the Loire Valley as you check out from your luxurious accommodation.

• Relish a seamless transfer to the grand Château de Versailles, near Paris, in our Mercedes Benz Class V vans.

• Embark on an awe-inspiring exploration of the opulent palace and its resplendent gardens, guided by experts.

• Unwind in luxury accommodations, allowing the essence of Versailles to envelop you.


Day 5: Excursion to Paris

• Embark on a remarkable day trip to Paris, the city of lights and love, in the comfort of our luxurious vans.

• Discover iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and more.

• Relish a gourmet lunch at a Michelin-starred restaurant, indulging in the finest culinary experiences Paris has to offer.

• Retreat to your accommodation near Versailles, cherishing the memorable day in the City of Love.


Day 6: Travel to German Castles

• Bid adieu to France as we journey to Germany in impeccable style, courtesy of Lex Luxe Transports.

• Arrive at the captivating German castle region, such as the Rhine Valley or Bavaria, feeling every bit a royal.


Day 7: German Castle Experience - Departure

• Savor a leisurely morning at your exquisite castle accommodation, relishing the serenity of your surroundings.

• Partake in a scenic boat cruise on the Rhine River (if in the Rhine Valley) or explore nearby German towns, further enhancing your experience.

• In the afternoon, our luxurious vans will ensure a seamless transfer to the airport or your preferred departure location, concluding the extraordinary Luxurious Castle Package.
