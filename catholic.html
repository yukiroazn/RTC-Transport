Here's an example of a pilgrimage Catholic tour package that includes visits to Lourdes, Fatima, and Lisbon :


Duration: 10 days

Destinations :

• Lourdes, France

• Fatima, Portugal

• Lisbon, Portugal


Day 1: Arrival in Lourdes

• Arrive in Lourdes and transfer to your hotel.

• Spend the day exploring the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Lourdes, including the Grotto of Massabielle, the Basilica of the Immaculate Conception, and the Baths.


Day 2: Lourdes

• Participate in the morning Mass and the Blessing of the Sick.

• Explore the Sanctuary's holy sites and attend the Candlelight Procession in the evening.


Day 3: Travel to Fatima

• Travel from Lourdes to Fatima by private transfer.

• Arrive in Fatima and check-in to your hotel.

• Attend the evening Candlelight Procession at the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima.


Day 4: Fatima

• Spend the day in Fatima, visiting the Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary, the Chapel of Apparitions, and the Monument to the Sacred Heart of Jesus.

• Attend Mass and participate in the Stations of the Cross.


Day 5: Fatima

• Optional day trip to Aljustrel, the birthplace of the shepherd children who witnessed the Marian apparitions.

• Visit the homes of Lucia, Francisco, and Jacinta, and gain insights into their lives.


Day 6: Travel to Lisbon

• Travel from Fatima to Lisbon by private transfer or train.

• Arrive in Lisbon and check-in to your hotel.

• Spend the evening exploring the historic neighborhoods of Lisbon.


Day 7: Lisbon

• Visit the Lisbon Cathedral and attend Mass.

• Explore other significant religious sites, such as the Basilica da Estrela and the Church of Santa Engrácia.


Day 8: Lisbon

• Discover the spiritual heritage of Lisbon with visits to the Jeronimos Monastery and the Church of St. Anthony.

• Enjoy free time to explore the city's cultural landmarks, such as the Belem Tower and the Alfama district.


Day 9: Lisbon

• Optional day trip to Santarém, known for the Eucharistic Miracle of the Holy Bread.

• Visit the Church of St. Stephen and the Church of the Holy Miracle.


Day 10: Departure

• Private transfer to the airport for your departure.


Please note that this is a sample itinerary, and you can customize it based on your preferences, available time, and budget. It's important to consider the spiritual significance of each destination and allocate sufficient time for prayer, reflection, and participation in religious ceremonies.
