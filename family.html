Here's an example of a family-friendly tour package that includes visits to Disneyland Paris, Center Parcs destinations, Europa-Park in Germany, and other kid-friendly activities nearParis:


Duration: 10 days

Destinations :

• Paris, France

• Disneyland Paris, France

• Center Parcs (choose one location), France

• Europa-Park, Germany


Arrival in Paris

• Arrive in Paris and transfer to your family-friendly hotel.

• Spend the day exploring the city's iconic landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.


Day 2-3: Disneyland Paris

• Embark on a magical adventure at Disneyland Paris.

• Explore both Disneyland Park and Walt Disney Studios Park, enjoying thrilling rides, enchanting shows, and meeting beloved Disney characters.

• Stay at one of the themed Disney hotels for an immersive experience.


Day 4-6: Center Parcs

• Travel to one of the Center Parcs locations near Paris, such as Les Trois Forêts or Le Lac d'Ailette.

• Enjoy a variety of family-friendly activities, including swimming in tropical water parks, cycling, nature walks, adventure playgrounds, and themed entertainment.

• Take time to relax and bond as a family in the peaceful and natural surroundings of the Center Parcs resort.


Day 7: Travel to Europa-Park

• Journey from France to Germany, heading to Europa-Park, one of Europe's most popular amusement parks.

• Spend the day exploring the park's themed areas, enjoying a wide range of attractions suitable for all ages.

• Stay at one of the Europa-Park's themed hotels for an immersive experience.


Day 8-9: Nearby Activities near Paris

• Return to Paris and explore kid-friendly activities in the city.

• Visit attractions like the Cité des Enfants at the Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie, the Jardin d'Acclimatation, or the Parc de la Villette.

• Enjoy family-friendly shows, museums, and parks that cater specifically to children.


Day 10: Departure

• Private transfer to the airport for your departure.


Please note that this is a sample itinerary, and you can customize it based on your family's preferences, available time, and budget. Consider the age of your children and their interests when selecting activities and attractions. Also, check the operating schedules of theme parks and attractions to ensure they align with your travel dates.
